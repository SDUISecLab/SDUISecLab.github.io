Reporter: Libo Chen

Time: 2021/09/26 19:00-23:00 (GMT+08:00) Beijing time

Chen Libo: Deputy Director of the Innovative Practice Teaching and Research Office of the School of Cyberspace Security, Shanghai Jiaotong University, graduated from the NISL laboratory of Tsinghua University, is a founding member of the famous security team "Blue-Lotus", with many years of network security offensive and defensive experience. He is mainly good at exploiting and exploiting vulnerabilities, penetration testing, and conducting research on frontier security technologies in IoT, blockchain and other fields. The research results of his team have not only been published in top international security conferences, but have also won many awards in vulnerability cracking contests, and have received acknowledgments from many well-known domestic and foreign manufacturers and hundreds of various vulnerability numbers such as CVE/CNVD/PSV. (CNCERT 2018 China Cyber Security Annual Conference-Smart Cracking Challenge Winning Award, "Tianfu Cup" 2018 Device Cracking Open Competition Winning Award, "Tianfu Cup" 2019 Best Original Vulnerability Recurrence Award, 2020 Great Vulnerability Challenge Cracking Success Award and Most Popularity Award), representative monographs include security bestseller "Metasploit Devil Training Camp", "Python Black Hat: Hacking and Penetration Testing Programming Method", etc.
