about us

We are a group of computer security researchers working at the Internet and Security Laboratory (ISecLab) within the School of Cyber Science and Technology at The Shandong University. Our mission is to solve real world security problems with cutting-edge research in network and computer security.

The problems of our interests include: (1) software security (e.g., binary code analysis and vulnerability discovery such as fuzzing)； (3) security and measurement for networking and large-scale distributed sytems ; （3）Security issues in the design and implementation of cryptography in real environments and (4) security in emerging computing platforms (e.g., mobile, IoT, AI, and cloud).
