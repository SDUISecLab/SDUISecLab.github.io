about us

A group of computer security researchers within the School of Cyber Science and Technology at Shandong University. Our mission is to solve real world security problems with cutting-edge research in network and computer security.

Problems of our interests include: (1)software security; (2)security and measurement for networking and large-scale distributed systems;(3)Security issues in the design and implementation of cryptography and (4)security in emerging computing platforms
