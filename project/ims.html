Internet Measurement and Security (IMS)

Without cyber security, there is no national security. Today, research on cyber security is still one of the most challenging tasks, especially for emerging network systems. Our research group is committed to the latest network-related technology or system security, e.g., Decentralized Cloud Storage system, Residential IP proxy system.
