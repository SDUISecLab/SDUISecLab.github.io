directions

S3 Lab is located in ECSS 4.214 of the Engineering and Computer Science South building (838 Franklyn Jenifer Dr, Richardson, TX 75080) in the center of the UT Dallas campus.

The closest parking garage is Parking Structure 4 (PS4), 2-3 blocks away from the ECSS building. The closest parking (surface) lot is Parking Lot J, which is slightly closer than the parking garage. Make sure your parking permit includes the parking lot that you will use. If you do not have a parking permit, you can use pay-per-use white-colored Visitor parking spaces at PS4. Use the parking machine near the east-end of the parking garage.
